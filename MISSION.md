# TaxSnax Mission & Vision

## Our Mission

**Make tax preparation accessible, organized, and stress-free for small business owners and independent workers.**

Tax season shouldn't be a scramble. Every year, millions of small business owners spend days wrangling bank statements, categorizing transactions, and trying to remember what that $47.32 charge was for. TaxSnax exists to transform this chaos into order—quickly, securely, and affordably.

We believe that organizing your finances for tax time should take **hours, not days**. That you shouldn't need a degree in accounting to understand your own business. That your sensitive information should be **protected by design, not by promise**.

## Our Vision

**A world where small business owners spend hours, not days, on tax preparation.**

We envision a future where:

- **Tax preparation is effortless** — Import, review, done. No all-nighters. No panic attacks in March.
- **Privacy is the default** — Tools don't ask for more than they need. SSN and EIN stay where they belong: secure and separate.
- **Automation is trustworthy** — AI that learns your business, understands your patterns, and gets smarter with every transaction.
- **Local-first is the standard** — Your financial data lives on your machine, not in someone's cloud. You own it. You control it.
- **CPAs are partners** — Technology organizes. Humans advise. Together they make tax season smooth.

## Core Beliefs

### 1. Privacy by Design

Most tax tools ask for everything. We ask for **only what's needed**.

- We don't store SSN because we don't need it to categorize expenses.
- We don't store EIN because we don't need it to calculate totals.
- We do store transaction data because that's what tax preparation is about.

**Security isn't a feature. It's the foundation.**

### 2. Local-First Data

Your financial history is yours. Not ours. Not Google's. Not some startup's.

- All data stays on your machine
- No cloud sync means no cloud breaches
- No API calls means no API outages
- Works offline, always

**Your data. Your machine. Your control.**

### 3. Human-in-the-Loop

Automation should assist, not replace.

- ML suggests categories—you confirm or correct
- Detects patterns—you decide what matters
- Organizes the chaos—you review before sending to CPA

**Technology handles the tedious. You handle the important.**

### 4. CPA Collaboration

We're not trying to replace accountants. We're trying to make their job easier.

- CPAs get clean, organized data
- You get professional advice and filing
- Everyone wins

**Organize with TaxSnax. Finalize with your CPA.**

## The Problem We're Solving

### The Current State

Every tax season, small business owners:
1. Download 12+ months of bank statements
2. Open Excel or Google Sheets
3. Manually categorize hundreds of transactions
4. Try to remember what each charge was for
5. Scramble to find contractor information
6. Realize they missed estimated payments
7. Pay rush fees to CPAs
8. Stress about whether they got it right

**Time spent: 20-40 hours. Stress level: Maximum.**

### The TaxSnax Way

With TaxSnax:
1. Import all statements (5 minutes)
2. Review auto-categorizations (15 minutes)
3. Generate CPA packet (1 minute)
4. Send to accountant
5. Relax

**Time spent: 1-2 hours. Stress level: Minimal.**
